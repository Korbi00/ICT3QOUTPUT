Can i change or cancel my Ovis Apparel order?

You can't make changes, but you can cancel an order within 30 minutes of placing it.

To cancel an order, first go to your orders page. If you're not signed in, you'll need to enter the order number from your confirmation email, along with your email address. Next, open the order you want to cancel, then tap or click the "Cancel" button and follow the prompts.

If you don't see the cancel button, it has been more than 30 minutes since your order was placed, and it can no longer be cancelled. But don't worryâ€”you can return the items within 30 days.

We can also help you cancel an order if it was placed within 30 minutes, just give us a call. Please note, we cannot change your delivery address or your delivery service for an existing order.

If you need to change an item's colour, size or quantity, or your delivery selection or address, simply cancel your original order and place a new one.

FAQs

How do refunds work with cancelled orders?

If you cancel an order, we'll automatically start processing your refund for most payment options (if payment was collected).
